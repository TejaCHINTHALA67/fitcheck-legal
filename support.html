Frequently Asked Questions

How do I add clothes to my wardrobe?

Tap the + button on the Closet tab, then take a photo or choose from your gallery. Our AI will automatically tag your item!

How does virtual try-on work?

First, upload a full-body photo of yourself in your Profile. Then select items from your closet and tap "Try On" to see how they look on you!

How do I cancel my subscription?

Subscriptions are managed through the App Store. Go to Settings → Your Name → Subscriptions → FitCheck AI → Cancel.

Can I get a refund?

Refunds are handled by Apple. Visit reportaproblem.apple.com to request a refund.

How do I delete my account?

Go to Profile → Settings → Delete Account. All your data will be permanently removed.
